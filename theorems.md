# The Eighteen Theorems

*This page is a placeholder. The theorem stack will be published here as part of the staged release.*

The eighteen theorems are derived from the five axioms and collectively cover:

- **Meaning emergence** from the questioning stance.
- **Saturation and collapse** of meaning-regimes under their own structural pressures.
- The **Inter-Layer Causal Interference Principle (ILCP)**: the mutual constraint between semantic and existential layers, and the characterization of regime collapse when the constraint is unilaterally violated.
- **Historical-phase transitions** and the three eschatons.
- **Self-generation of meaning** under the unique-subject axiom, and the derivation of the §6 action principle.
